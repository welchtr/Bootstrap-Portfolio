About Me

Tiara is the production and billing manger for a liberal political multimedia production company called SWAY.

She is a Washington D.C native who received her Bachelor of Arts in journalism from Miami University of Ohio.

Her background in production ranges from serving as an on-set production assistant to video editing.

Since joining the SWAY team in 2016, Tiara has administered over 80 video shoots in about 20 cities in 12 states including Washington D.C, New York, Florida, Illinois, Iowa, New Mexico, and California. Beyond administrative support, she has aided SWAY in the completion of over 100 projects that includes television, digital, and radio ads.
